Guess the Number

Make a guess between 1 and 100

To make game more exciting please specify your own minimum and maximum numbers

Instructions:
A random number will be generated from 1 to 100 upon entering the site and you will need to guess it. If you are two high or low a prompt will inform you and you can guess accordingly. If you guess a value outside of the number it will inform you it is invalid and you need to guess within range.

After you guess the correct number the minimum and maximum number will decrease and increase, respectively, by a factor of 10. If at anytime you want to reset the game just select the reset button and you will be return to a minimum number of 1 and a maximum number of 100. If you entered a number in the guess box and want to clear this number, feel free to either you your backspace key or hit the clear button to clear the field.

You can also enter your own minimum and maximum numbers in the the range below the guess. You will need to make sure that the number enter in the left box is lower than the number entered in the right box for your numbers to be submitted. When you submit your own number range, the random number will be selected from this new range.
Lastly have fun and enjoy killing some time playing the game!
